## Risk Management

### Potential Risks
1. **Payment Issues**: Stripe integration complexity
   - *Mitigation*: Thorough testing, sandbox environment
   
2. **Game Balance**: Slot games too easy/hard
   - *Mitigation*: Playtesting, adjustable RTP rates
   
3. **Server Load**: High traffic during arcade play
   - *Mitigation*: Load balancing, caching, CDN
   
4. **Security**: Payment data and user info protection
   - *Mitigation*: HTTPS, PCI compliance, regular audits
