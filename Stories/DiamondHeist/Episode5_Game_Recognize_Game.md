# Episode 5: Game Recognize Game

**Perspective:** King Pimpin's POV  
**Project:** Pimpire Chronicles  
**Setting:** The Control Room - King watches Diamond beat his arcade traps

---

## Context

King Pimpin' is in his high-tech control room, watching Diamond navigate the traps we saw her beat in her story.

---

## PAGE 1: The Slot Machine (The Bet)

### Panel 1 (Page 1)

**Visual:** King Pimpin' is sitting in a massive gaming chair. He is holding a bucket of popcorn. On the main screen, we see a live feed of Diamond standing in front of the Slot Machine Door.

**Yago:** "She's at the Jackpot Door, Boss! Odds of her cracking the encryption? 10,000 to 1. She's gonna trigger the trap door!"

### Panel 2 (Page 1)

**Visual:** On the screen, Diamond pulls out her jamming device (the mirror).

**King Pimpin':** "Don't bet against the house, Yago. Watch her hands."

**Visual:** The screen flashes 7-7-7. The "WINNER" lights go off in the control room.

**SFX:** Ding! Ding! Ding!

**Visual:** King Pimpin' tosses a piece of popcorn into his mouth, grinning.

### Panel 1 (Page 2)

**Visual:** Diamond enters the "Deep Sea" hallway on the screen. King Pimpin' leans forward, grabbing a joystick built into his chair armrest.

**King Pimpin':** "Alright, let's turn up the difficulty. Yago, deploy the Shark Squadron."

### Panel 2 (Page 2)

**King Pimpin':** "Dip. Duck. Dive. Come on, baby girl, show me your footwork."

**Visual:** On the screen, Diamond throws her high heel at the ceiling emitter. The feed flickers with static for a second.

**Visual:** King Pimpin' laughs out loud. He is having the time of his life.
