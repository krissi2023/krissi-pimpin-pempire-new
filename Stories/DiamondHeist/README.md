# The Pimpire Chronicles: Diamond Heist Arc

**Genre:** Urban / Heist / Comedy / Romance  
**Target Audience:** Arcade Gamers & Comic Readers

---

## THE LOGLINE

A master thief named Diamond tries to rob the Pimpire Penthouse, only to realize the owner, King Pimpin', has turned his security system into a high-stakes arcade game. It's a battle of wits, seduction, and high scores.

---

## CHARACTERS

### DIAMOND (The Player)

- **Role:** Protagonist / Thief
- **Vibe:** "Classy on the streets, Thug in the sheets."
- **Visuals:** Starts in elegant evening gowns that tear away into tactical gear
- **Personality:** Educated, polite, and innocent on the surface; manipulative, skilled, and street-smart underneath

### KING PIMPIN' (The Game Master)

- **Role:** Antagonist / Love Interest / The House
- **Vibe:** "The smartest guy in the room." Smooth, wealthy, unbothered
- **Visuals:** Sharp suits, sunglasses, control room aesthetics
- **Personality:** He isn't trying to arrest her; he's trying to test her. He respects the hustle

### YAGO (The Glitch)

- **Role:** Digital Sidekick / Comic Relief / Paid Bonus Content
- **Visuals:** A holographic avatar (robot or stylized street character) that appears on screens and phones
- **Personality:** A snitch. High anxiety. Constantly calculating odds and complaining when things break

---

## GAME MECHANICS (THE TRAPS)

1. **The Slot Machine Door:** Security lock based on spinning reels. Requires "Jackpot" to enter
2. **The Fish Table Lasers:** Holographic sharks and whales that move in arcade patterns
3. **The Card Floor:** A pressure-sensitive floor looking like playing cards. Must walk the "Royal Flush" to survive
